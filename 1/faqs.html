Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase. Please ensure the item is unused and in original packaging.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, we will email you a tracking number. You can use that on the carrier's website to track the delivery.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or canceled within 1 hour of placing them. Please contact us immediately.
